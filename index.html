Our Story

Built for this neighborhood. Made for everyone.

Third Time Together started with a simple idea: that a café can be a real gathering place — not a chain, not a concept, but somewhere that feels like yours. We make everything from scratch, pull flavors from cultures we love and respect, and believe that every meal is worth showing up for.

We're at 399 Binney St in Kendall Square, open all day, every day.

Getting Here

Finding us is the easy part.

🏢
Garage Parking — We Validate

Park at One Kendall Square Garage. Ask any staff member for a validation slip — and hold onto your ticket, you'll need it to re-enter through the pedestrian entrance on your way back to the garage.

🚗
Street Parking

Metered street parking available on Binney St and surrounding streets in Kendall Square.

🚇
MBTA Red Line

Kendall/MIT stop — 5 minute walk down Binney St. We're at 399 Binney.

Bike & Bluebikes
